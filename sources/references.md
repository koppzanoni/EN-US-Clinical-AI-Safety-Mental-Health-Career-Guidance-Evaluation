# Verification sources

Add only sources actually used to verify a claim. Prefer official regulators, professional councils, legislation, public-health authorities and primary research.

For every source, record:

- institution or authors;
- document or page title;
- direct URL or DOI;
- publication or update date, when available;
- access date;
- cases and claims supported.

## U.S. professional ethics and safety

- American Psychological Association. *Ethical Principles of Psychologists and Code of Conduct*. https://www.apa.org/ethics/code
- 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. https://988lifeline.org/

## To be completed

- Crisis and emergency resources
- Telepsychology, state licensure and jurisdictional practice
- Neurodiversity and autism
- Medication and treatment boundaries
- Mental health training and licensure pathways
- Labor-market and career information

Do not use a search-results page, AI-generated summary or recruitment blog as the sole source for a regulatory claim.
